# Code of Conduct

Lantern Protocol is committed to a professional, respectful, and technically serious collaboration environment.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas, designs, code, and evidence without personal attacks.
- Assume good faith while still requiring rigor.
- Keep discussions focused on project goals, security, reliability, and maintainability.
- Respect privacy, disclosure boundaries, and sensitive operational context.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal abuse.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussions.
- Encouraging unsafe, illegal, or destructive activity.
- Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities before maintainers have had a fair opportunity to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content; close issues; restrict participation; or escalate security-sensitive matters as needed to protect the project and community.
